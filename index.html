The purpose of this blog is encorage learning-unlearning-relearning at any age trying new approaches.

Let me show you my learning strategy with kids

Propitiate

An environment that stimulates learning and arouses curiosity.

A routine with times dedicated to study.

Observe

Maintain an observer role of the activities, assisting in case there are doubts or support only if is required.

Experience

Try differents activities: science, music, movies, reading, plays, crafts, etc.

Facing difficulties can lead us to develop in areas that we never imagined.
